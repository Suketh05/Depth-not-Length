# SWE-ContextBench — the paper's reported leaderboard

> **Source of truth.** All numbers here are transcribed VERBATIM from the paper
> *"Depth, Not Length."* These are the paper's **reported** results, not regenerated by
> this repo's harness. CSV: [`data/swe_context_bench.csv`](data/swe_context_bench.csv).

SWE-ContextBench ([arXiv:2602.08316](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.08316)): 1,476 real
coding tasks across 51 repositories with test-suite execution. The paper reports two
framings:

- **Headline (unified harness, `tab:stdbench`):** Brief task resolution **47.3%**, next
  best **Unabyss 37.6%**. This feeds
  [`data/paper_public_benchmarks.csv`](data/paper_public_benchmarks.csv).
- **Leaderboard with token cost (`tab:t070`):** below. Brief's row is flagged **modeled**
  in the paper (the `tab:t070` caption reads "partial sourcing + Brief (modeled)"); the
  other rows are published competitor numbers.
